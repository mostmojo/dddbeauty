Treatments

A treatment that helps to improve old scars and imperfections of the skin, restoring collagen and elastin through a natural healing process. Ultrafine needles, with which multiple cutaneous punctures are made that induce a healing response of the skin producing new collagen and elastin.

A treatment that generates the growth of new fibroblasts by reproduction from autologue small cells & fibroblasts, using a special rejuvenation solution and an innovative drug delivery system.
